Here is a short history of technology growth in bullets :

- 1822 - Charles Babbage conceptualizes the first mechanical computer, the Difference Engine. - 1876 - Alexander Graham Bell invents the telephone, revolutionizing real-time communication. - 1890 - The first electric streetlights are installed in Wabash, Indiana, marking the beginning of widespread electrification. - 1906 - Lee de Forest invents the vacuum tube, a crucial component in early computers and radios. - 1943 - The first electronic computer, ENIAC (Electronic Numerical Integrator and Computer), is developed. - 1947 - The transistor is invented, leading to smaller, faster, and more efficient electronics. - 1951 - The first commercial computer, UNIVAC I, is released. - 1958 - The integrated circuit is developed, further miniaturizing electronics. - 1969 - The first humans walk on the moon, marking a major achievement in space technology- 1971 - The first microprocessor, the Intel 4004, is released. - 1973 - The first cell phone call is made. - 1981 - The first personal computer, the IBM PC, is released. - 1983 - The Internet Protocol (IP) is developed, laying the foundation for modern internet communication. - 1989 - The World Wide Web is invented by Tim Berners-Lee. - 1991 - The first smartphone, the IBM Simon, is released. - 1998 - Google is founded, revolutionizing search and online advertising. - 2007 - The first iPhone is released, popularizing touchscreen smart phones. - 2010 - The first iPad is released, popularizing tablets. - 2011 - Cloud computing becomes mainstream, allowing for on-demand access to computing resources. - 2019 - 5G wireless networks begin rolling out, offering faster data speeds and lower latency. Note: This is not an exhaustive list, but rather a selection of significant milestones in the history of technology growth. If you want more information please visit wiki
