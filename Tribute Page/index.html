Here is a short history of technology growth in bullets : 

-  1822 - Charles Babbage conceptualizes the first mechanical computer, the Difference Engine.

- 1876 - Alexander Graham Bell invents the telephone, revolutionizing real-time communication.

- 1890 - The first electric streetlights are installed in Wabash, Indiana, marking the beginning of widespread electrification.

- 1906 - Lee de Forest invents the vacuum tube, a crucial component in early computers and radios.
